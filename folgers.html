Folgers Coffee | Rebrand

This project is an approach to rebrand Folgers coffee to give it more current and updated packaging while also keeping sustainability at the centre of the rebrand. The orginal package of Folgers is a bulky, plastic container but the rebrand focusses on creating a paper package that is 100% recyclable and takes up less space to store in one's pantry

I used the following tools and software to create this piece:
— Adobe InDesign
— Adobe Illustrator
— Adobe Photoshop
— Lots of credible research!!
