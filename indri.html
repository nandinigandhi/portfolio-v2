Indri

I wanted to try to create something out of my comfort zone and I landed on rebranding the bottle of an Indian Whiskey company named Indri. The word 'Indri' in Sanskrit refers to the five senses: touch, taste, sight, sound, and smell. I wanted to create a bottle that encompassed all five senses and created a sensory experience.

Here are the tools and software I used to create this piece of work:
— Adobe InDesign
— Adobe Illustrator
— Blender

I have included a lot of bottles that I created before reaching the final bottle to show process through iteration and how I approached the final form.
